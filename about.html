About the Artist

MURO Studio — Where Walls Become Worlds

MURO
The Artist Behind the Wall

Every wall is a blank page. For me, painting is not just about decoration — it's about dialogue. I believe that public art has the power to transform neighborhoods and inspire conversations that wouldn't happen otherwise.

Born in the streets and refined in the studio, my style is a blend of traditional fine art techniques and modern street art rebellion. With nearly a decade of experience, I've had the privilege of transforming spaces across 18 cities on 4 continents.

My process starts with listening — understanding the community, the architecture, and the story a space is trying to tell. Then I translate that energy into color, scale, and emotion that resonates for decades.

Each mural is a collaboration between artist, environment, and audience. When the scaffolding comes down, what remains is something that belongs to everyone.

MURO

Skills & Mediums

Fresco Spray Art Oil-Based Acrylic Digital Projection Mapping Latex Eco Paint

Philosophy

Art for Everyone

Public art should be accessible, inclusive, and free to experience. Murals break the barrier between art and everyday life.

Spaces that Speak

Architecture has a voice. My job is to amplify it — to give blank walls a narrative that connects with the community.

Craft Over Commerce

Every project is handcrafted with intention. Quality, longevity, and artistic integrity always take precedence over speed.

The Journey

2016
First commissioned piece — a 30ft rooftop mural in Mumbai's Bandra district that caught the attention of local press.
2018
International debut — invited to paint a 4-story building in Berlin's Kreuzberg neighborhood for the Urban Nation festival.
2020
Launched MURO Studio — a dedicated space for concept development, community workshops, and collaborative projects.
2022
Won the "Urban Visionary" award at the International Street Art Festival. Completed 100th mural milestone.
2024
Completed the largest single-wall mural in the state — a 12,000 sq ft piece celebrating local heritage and environmental sustainability.
2025
Expanding into digital projection mapping and eco-friendly paint technologies. 120+ murals across 18 cities and counting.
