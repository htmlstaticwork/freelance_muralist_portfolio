Custom Commissions

Your vision, amplified. One-on-one collaboration for bespoke wall art.

Your Story, Our Canvas

A custom commission is the most personal form of mural work. It begins with a conversation — about your memories, your dreams, the textures and colors that move you — and evolves into a one-of-a-kind artwork that exists nowhere else in the world.

Whether you're commemorating a milestone, honoring cultural heritage, or simply want a wall that sparks joy every day — I bring the technical craft and creative intuition to make it happen. Every stroke is intentional, every detail deliberate.

Commissions range from intimate indoor pieces to large exterior installations. No two projects are alike — and that's the point.

What's Included

Deep Consultation

Extended discovery session to understand your narrative and aesthetic preferences.

Multiple Concepts

Three unique concept directions with full color studies for you to choose from.

Revision Rounds

Two rounds of revisions included to ensure the design is exactly right.

Material Selection

Guided selection of paints, finishes, and textures tailored to your environment.

Certificate

Signed certificate of authenticity and care instructions for your artwork.

Nationwide Delivery

Available for on-site installation across India. International projects considered.
