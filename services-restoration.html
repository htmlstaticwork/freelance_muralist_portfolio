Restoration & Preservation

Honoring the past while protecting art for the future.

Breathing New Life Into Heritage

Murals weather, fade, and deteriorate over time. Restoration is not about repainting — it's about understanding the original technique, matching the materials, and carefully repairing damage while maintaining the authentic character of the artwork.

With training in both contemporary street art and traditional fresco techniques, I bring a unique perspective to restoration projects. I've worked on everything from 50-year-old heritage murals to decade-old public art pieces damaged by weather and urban pollution.

Every restoration begins with a thorough assessment — documenting the original condition, identifying structural issues, and creating a preservation plan that balances faithfulness to the original with long-term durability.

What's Included

Condition Report

Detailed photographic documentation and written assessment of current damage.

Material Matching

Careful selection of paints and techniques that match the original work.

Structural Repair

Wall preparation, crack filling, and surface stabilization before any paint work.

Color Restoration

Expert color matching to bring faded areas back to their original vibrancy.

Protective Seal

Anti-graffiti, UV-resistant clear coating for long-term preservation.

Documentation

Before/after report with maintenance guide for ongoing preservation.
